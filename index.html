About Me

My name is Sonny Carton. Ever since I was born I was obssesed with sports. From watching and talking about sports to playing them I could never get enough.

I have three siblings, but my younger brother Lucky is who I really developed my love of sports alongside. He was two years younger than me, but that gap was small enough that we competed against each other in everything.

I was born on May 31, 2002 in a small town in Pennslyvania called Huntingdon Valley.

I lived here up until I was 9 years old and my family and I moved to New York City. It was here that my journey into the world of broadcasting began.
