New York Knicks History

The Knicks became an official NBA team in 1949. They were one of the founding members of the BAA, which ended up merging with the NBL to form the NBA

Their first ever coach was named Joe Lapchick and their leading scorer was Carl Braun, a Point Guard from Brooklyn
